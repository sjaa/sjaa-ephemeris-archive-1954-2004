Minutes of Jan 7 Planning Meeting

Meeting held at John LeConey home

Board of Director Meeting held prior
to membership planning meeting.

Board of Director voted formal acceptance
of new members Eli Levinson and carpenter.

Board decided to call for vote of acceptance
on newly written by-Laws.

Rob Ferguson announced that Olan
Serachiney has a friend who will build
a scope tube at 1¢ cost plus no labor(s.s)

The Board discussed vote of thanks to
T. Miller.

The advisability of writing a letter
to the general land usage of permanent
in order to obtain an available 24"
mgor of clan. It was discussed.
Bob Ferguson was instructed to write
the letter and the offer was accepted.

Walter Kleiman proposed inviting a
professional speaker to our meetings.
Board also discussed inviting a high
Observatory professor to speak on
"Observation and possibly show a film
on this topic.

Y. Phelps was detailed to contact
Hy Gingrove concerning the possibility
of using a room suitable for showing
a film to 150 people.

Walter Kleiman proposed displaying a
wall chart as appeared in January issue of Sky

October 24, 1957

Star Gazers Search For Satellite

Three local amateur astronomers are shown above scanning the heavens for Russia's -ed.

satellite, Sputnik. Pictured are Carl Hart, Ira Randall and Frank Galvin. The sky watchers couldn't spot the satellite at the time this picture was ◊ ◊ ◊

taken, but they haven't given up yet. The young assistant is David Hart, father's helper.
