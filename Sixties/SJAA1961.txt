1/23/61

Astronomy Society
To See Space Film

Project Mercury, America's plan to put a man into space, will be the subject of a movie at the Feb. 6 meeting of the San Jose Amateur Astronomers at 8 p.m. in the Moorpark Recreation Building, Moorpark and S. Monroe avenues.

The film was prepared by Ames Laboratory at Moffett Field.
