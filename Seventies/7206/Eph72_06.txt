JUNE 2, BOARD ELECTIONS AND EQUIPMENT NIGHT
The June meeting is election night. Board members are elected for two year terms. On even years, such as 1972, four board positions must be filled. On odd years five positions are open. The following persons have positions expiring as of June 2, 1972:   Ralph Bridge 252- 9396   Dick Glasspool 286-9843

Other persons interested in board positions but not listed should contact Dave Erickson at 269-4838 or at a meeting.

As well as election of the board there will be an equipment night. This is your chance to show and discuss your new or old pieces of astronomical equipment. All members and guests are urged to come and bring their astro-things to discuss. People who bring things should be prepared to discuss their function "briefly". The meeting will be held at Community Bank Building, Market & St. John Streets, second floor executive conference room, at 8 P.M. For information or directions call Dave at 269-4858.

JUNE 9, BOARD MEETING AT COMMUNITY BANK  8 P.M.
The June 9th board meeting is where the officers for the next year will be elected. Each board member has one vote for each official position. Officers are selected from the general membership, the board and current officers. Each board member casts his vote for each position. Officers are then installed at the Annual Banquet on July 16.

Following officer elections banquet preparations will be discussed. All members and guests are welcome to attend.

**SAN JOSE AMATEUR'S ANNUAL**
BANQUET & INSTALLATION OF OFFICERS
*JULY 16, 1972*

The Red Carpet Restaurant is hosting the banquet. Members and
guests with families or friends are invited to attend.

Dr. Witteborn, a lifetime amateur astronomer and now head
of NASA's "Airborn Astronomy," will speak. His topic, "Infra-
Red Astronomy From Above."

At six o'clock there will be a happy hour, followed by dinner
at seven o'clock. Possible entrees are Prime Rib and Chicken
Kiev. Tickets will sell for around six dollars. For dinner
reservations contact Dave at the regular meeting or by mail,
(4112 Camden Ave., S.J., 95124). For more information contact
banquet chairman Dr. Gregory at 268-3095. It's an excellent
night out that you won't want to miss.
