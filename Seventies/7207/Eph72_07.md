## * * San Jose Amateur Astronomers * * Annual Banquet and Installation of Officers * July 16, 1972 *

July 16th is the big night. The banquet will take the place of the July meeting, and will be located at the Red Carpet Restaurant in Eastridge. At six o'clock there will be a Happy Hour, followed at seven o'clock by dinner. The dinner is a five course meal, including soup, salad, roast beef, dessert and beverage. For reservations write David Erickson, 4112 Camden Ave, San Jose, or Dr. A.B. Gregory, 5480 Fairway Dr., San Jose, 95127. Reservations must be made no later than July 10, 1972. The price is $6.25.

## PLANNING MEETING

The meeting will be at Dave Aguilar's home, 4677 Scotts Valley Dr., Scotts Valley. (Phone 438-0428) The meeting will start at eight p.m.

## STARPARTY

There will be a starparty July 8 at the Skyline site, which is located 1.8 miles past Skyline Blvd. (toward Felton) on Hwy 9. The clearing is on the left.
