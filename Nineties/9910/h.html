Autumn Astronomy Day


Autumn Astronomy day will once again be celebrated by Northern California clubs. The SJAA will hold it's regularly scheduled star party on Friday October 15, and the final Public Program of the season will be held at Fremont Peak on Saturday October 16th.

For more information, visit the AANC website at www.lhs.berkeley.edu/sii/aanc/AstroDay.html
