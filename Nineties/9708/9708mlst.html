New SJAA EMail List and Forum

Due to the efforts of Bill Arnett and the good graces of SEDS, SJAA now has a subscription email list.

We will be transmitting the Ephemeris each month for those who would also like to have a text copy. It will also be useful for notification of current events that can't get into the Ephemeris due to time constraints, and discussion of ideas and issues that come up in relation to the club.

SJAA members can subscribe by sending an email message to:

    sjaa-request@seds.org
with the single word "subscribe" (no quotes) in the body of the message.

Bill Arnett, Scott Wade; last updated: 1997 July 21
