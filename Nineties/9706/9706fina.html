SWAP Team Results - (GOOD!)

Figures Supplied By Treasurer Bob Elsberry

for the SJAA  Swap Meet and Auction last month

Attendance was good, and this was a slightly better financial result than last year. It's a strong indication of the continuing success of this popular event.  Congratulations to everyone who participated both in preparing the event and attending, with a special thanks to Jay Freeman for his entertaining auctioneering, so enthusiastic that he convinced himself to bid on (and purchase) several items.
     Swap Meet    $610.35
     Auction      $160.90
     Soda Sales    $24.90
                    
     TOTAL        $796.15
