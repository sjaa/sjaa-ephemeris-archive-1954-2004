Aldebaran Graze: Negative Result, Good Night

Bill Arnett

SJAA members Bill O'Shaughnessy, David R. Smith, Paul Barton and I met in Gilroy to observe the Aldebaran graze the evening of July 29. We had some nice views of Jupiter while waiting for the Big Event which was scheduled for 2:40am.

Unfortunately, the Moon and Aldebaran were only 3.5 degrees above the eastern horizon at that time. They didn't rise above the cloud bank to our east until 2:50. We missed it by 10 minutes or about 2 degrees.

Oh well, it was still a nice night under the stars.

Bill Arnett; last updated: 1997 Sept 12
