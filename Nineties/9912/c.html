Party Time at Houge


Come to Houge Park December 18 and help celebrate the arrival of the Winter Solstice. At regular meeting time this month, we'll be hosting a pot luck party. Bring any and every kind of food or beverage in any quantity. Silly astro themes are encouraged. We'll provide coffee.

In addition, we will host the Silly Astro Gift Exchange. Bring something relatively useless and weird, but wrapped. Participation is strictly optional.

And by all means, bring telescopes, astro gadgets, and photos to share with the group.
