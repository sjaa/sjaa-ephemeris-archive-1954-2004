Chabot Auction Results

Denni Medlock


Figures are in: Approximately $18,000 was raised, 20% of which goes to the Telescope Makers Workshop at Chabot, which plans to use it to buy grit and mirror blanks for those who can't normally afford those goodies.

(Since the workshop is the closest thing to an anarchy I know, with no structure or membership dues, this type of funding is a miracle.) Many thanks to all who attended, and for those of you who didn't - you missed a good one.

But don't be surprised to find a lot of this stuff recycling through the SJAA Auction and RTMC next year. I think Joe Sunseri single-handedly supported the auction....
