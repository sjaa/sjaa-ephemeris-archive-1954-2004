Yosemite National Park Star Party

Jim Van Nuland


The SJAA weekend at Yosemite National Park comes early this year, June 27-28.  Astronomically, it's not a good choice, as the nights are short:  Friday a 41% moon rises at 1:24 am; Saturday a 30% moon rises at 2 am.

On the positive side, Yosemite should be excellent, with the falls still active.  With the damage last winter, parking will be restricted, and you may not get into the park without a reservation.

Soooo, the star party gives you a reservation in the group camp at Bridalveil Creek Campground.  And you get all of Yosemite for free. When you consider that many people travel 10,000 miles to see it, we can't complain.
