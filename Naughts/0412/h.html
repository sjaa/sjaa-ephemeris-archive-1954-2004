Cassini Lecture at Foothill College

  

January 26, 2005
7 P.M.
Dr. Jeff Cuzzi of NASA Ames will give a talk on the Cassini mission on January 26 at Foothill College. Since this talk occurs less than 2 weeks after the Huygens probe lands on Titan, it promises to be a very interesting discussion. More details in the January Ephemeris or call 650-949-7888.
