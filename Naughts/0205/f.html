Annual SJAA Yosemite Trip

Jim Van Nuland

  

The annual SJAA Yosemite star party will be held on July 5 and 6, at Glacier Point. Up to 30 people will be given free admission and camping, in exchange for two public events on Friday and Saturday evenings. We are expected to have at least 1 scope per 2 people.

This year we will share the space with the Central Valley Astronomers, from Fresno.

Various information may be found on my Yosemite Page, http://www.svpal.org/~jvn/yosemite.htm
