Jack Zeiders Donates Telescope and Big Binocular

Jim Van Nuland

  

Jack Zeiders has donated his 17-inch dobsonian telescope to SJAA. Many of you have admired this scope and it's remarkable design and workmanship, which compares very favorably to such makers as Obsession.

In addition, he donated a 1940's Japanese battleship binocular with 6-inch objectives. This is interesting for its history, as well as its superb optics.

We thank Jack for this latest generosity.
