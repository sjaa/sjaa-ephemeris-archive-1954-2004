Project Astro South Bay Partners Needed

Bob Havner

  

Karin Avila, the Project Astro coordinator, needs astronomer partners for the Peninsula and San Jose areas. I am a Project Astro partner astronomer and my own personal experience with the program has been great! It is a great way to share what you know with young people in our community. For information about Project Astro go to: http://www.astrosociety.org/education/astro/project_astro.html
