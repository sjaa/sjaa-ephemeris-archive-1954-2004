Andrah Foundation supports SJAA

As in previous years, the Andrah Foundation of Michigan, through it's local representatives, Paul and Ann Summers, have given $600 to SJAA. This is to promote education and public awareness of Astronomy. We thank Paul and Ann, and the Andrah Foundation, for their generosity.
