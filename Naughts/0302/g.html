Family Astro event leader training

Kristin Nelson

  

The Astronomical Society of the Pacific would like to invite all amateur and professional astronomers to participate in the Family ASTRO Moon mission event leader training on February 8th (9:30 am - 2:30 pm) at SFSU.

Family ASTRO trains teachers, astronomers, and museum and community group educators to help children and adults explore astronomy together. Participants will learn to lead family astronomy events focusing on the Moon. You do not need to have a family to become an event leader! The training is free!

For more information or to apply, please visit our website http://www.astrosociety.org/education/family.html or contact Kristin Nelson, Bay Area coordinator at knelson@astrosociety.org or 415-337-1100 ext 101.
