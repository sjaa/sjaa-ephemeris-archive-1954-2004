Volunteer to Help KTEH
August 16

Mike Koop

The SJAA has been requested to provide volunteer support answering phones for the KTEH pledge drive on Thursday August 16th from 6:30 p.m.-11:30 p.m. During this shift, Best of Cosmos, and Odyssey: The Mind's Eye will be showing for approximately 3 hours. Food is provided and our club will receive on-air credit for their contributions of time during their volunteer shift if we provide 10 or more people. Please contact Mike Koop (WK: (408)473-6315) or via email (koopm@best.com) if you are interested in helping out.
