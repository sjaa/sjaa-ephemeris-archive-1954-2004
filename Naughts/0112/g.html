Flea Market Results

Gary Mitchell

The SJAA made $216.50 in commissions at the swap/flea market Sunday. In addition, we received $55 for that donated telescope tube and accessories, (and we have a happy V.P. since now he doesn't have to store it at his place). Grand total: $271.50.
