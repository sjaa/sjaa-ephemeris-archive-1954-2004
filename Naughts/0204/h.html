Loaner Notes

Mike Koop

  

The SJAA would like to thank Peggy Bernard for the generous donation of her 10" Orion Deep Space Explorer. For those who didn't know, Peggy is a member of the SJAA and one of our professional amateur astronomers. After an injury she received while working at Lick Observatory and the subsequent knee surgery she was unable to handle the "Big Dob." She is now recovering from her surgery and we all hope to see her soon at SJAA events.
