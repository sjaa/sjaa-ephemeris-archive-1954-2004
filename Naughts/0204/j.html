AANC Conference May 18, 2002

Jane Houston Jones

  

The AANC Conference will be held at and co-sponsored by the College of San Mateo this year. Registration, agenda, box lunch selection forms and directions can be found on the AANC website, www.aanc-astronomy.org.

Talks will range from the Kepler Mission, to study terrestrial planets orbiting stars, to a talk on the black hole in our own galaxy, to the DART membrane telescope to yet another presentation featuring a representative from Coronado Filters.

At lunch view the sun through various solar filters, while enjoying your selection of box lunches.

After the day of talks, stick around for a social hour and planetarium shows (also at lunchtime) followed by an old fashioned spaghetti feed brought to you by the San Mateo County Astronomical Society, followed by a star party.
