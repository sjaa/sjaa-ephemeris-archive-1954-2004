So long, and thanks for all the starfish

Morris Jones and Jane Houston Jones

  

We've been editing the SJAA Ephemeris since February 1999. A lot has happend since then. I married the co-editor. Two of our columnists got married too! and now we are passing on the editorial baton to another sorta newly-married couple, Paul and Mary Kohlmiller!

I know you will continue to send Paul and Mary your interesting tales of eclipse chases, astro and other photos and articles you think will interest the readersip of the Ephemeris. It's been a lot of fun for us. We'll now enjoy reading the Ephemeris every month rather than editing it.
