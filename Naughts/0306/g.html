Donation to SJAA in memory of Rita Miram

Jim Van Nuland

  

Bill Dellinges, a SJAA member now living and observing in Arizona, honored the memory of SJAA member and Sacramento resident Rita Miram with a donation to the SJAA. Bill's generosity and thoughfulness spans the miles that separate us.

SJAA thanks Bill for his generosity. See July 2002 Ephemeris for announcement.
