CalStar 2003

 
Join this group at CalStar2003, September 25, 26. 27 2003

This year's CalStar, hosted by the SJAA, takes place September 25, 26, and 27, 2003 at Lake San Antonio. You can get all the information you need on the Calstar website: http://www201.pair.com/resource/calstar/

Basically, it is an end of the season star party. No speakers, no prizes, nothing but astronomy and friends. Lake San Antonio is halfway between Los Angeles and the San Francisco Bay Area. Park fee is $32.00 per vehicle for the three nights, $16. 00 for an optional stay on Sunday night. Campsites, RV sites with full hookup, and even some rental cabins are available in the park.
