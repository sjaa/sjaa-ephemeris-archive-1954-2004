Learn to be an ATM

  

Learn to make a telescope! Class begins Thursday, February 7, at 7:30 p.m. at Houge Park. It's easier than you think and a lot of fun! See the December issue of the SJAA Ephemeris for more information. (Oh, and ATM means "amateur telescope maker.")
