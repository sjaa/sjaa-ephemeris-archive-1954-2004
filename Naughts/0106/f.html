Santa Cruz Observing

Chris Angelos

The Santa Cruz Astronomy Club is inviting SJAA members to attend the club's regularly scheduled observing nights at Bonny Doon Airfield. Sessions for June are scheduled on the 16th and 23rd.

Ordinarily, Bonny Doon Airfield is off limits to the general public because it is private property. However, the owner has graciously allowed us to use the location for observing one or two nights a month. Try to arrive about sunset. Find directions to Bonny Doon Airfield at the SCAC web site http://astro.santa-cruz.ca.us/.

Club news and schedules are updated on the club message center, 831-335-2423.
