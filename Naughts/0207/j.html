Rita Miram, -2002

Harry Miram

  

I would like to announce that my wife of 50 years Rita Miram, a long time member of SJAA whose health did not permit to be active in Astronomy, passed away on Sunday, June 9, 2002. For the last several years she just loved to read about the club and study Sky and Telescope. Six months ago we moved to the Sacramento area.

Sincerely, Harry Miram
