SJAA Receives Cash Award

Jim Van Nuland

The Andrah Foundation has donated $550 to SJAA for support of education and public awareness of astronomy. Since nearly all of our programs are educational, the direction will be easy to follow.

Thank you to SJAA members Paul and Ann Summers, who are the local representatives of the Foundation.
