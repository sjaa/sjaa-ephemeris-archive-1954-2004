Additions to Loaner Scope Program

Mike Koop

Orion Telescope held a "sidewalk sale" on the last weekend of August, and the SJAA took the opportunity to purchase two new Orion dobsonian telescopes at a deep discount. The new XT6 and XT8 telescopes are now in the loaner program.

Orion also donated various telescope caps and covers.

Special thanks to Tim Gieseler and Jack Kellythorn of Orion Telescope for their support of the SJAA loaner program!
