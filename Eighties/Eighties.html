The Eighties

1980

    First A.B. Gregory Award presented to Kevin Medlock. Star Parties on Mt. Umunhum.

1981

    First Annual Astronomical Auction. First Space Shuttle Flight.

1982

    Astronomy Day at De Anza College and K-mart

1983

    Public Star Party at Marriot's Great America shows 14,000 people Astronomical Objects!

1984

    First Yosemite Star Party! Construction Started on Fremont Peak Observatory.

1985

    SJAA gets Tax Exempt Status! Don Discovers his second Comet at RTMC!

1986

    Return of Halley's Comet! Public Star Parties at Branham Lane. FPOA Opens.

1987

    Computer Controlled Telescope Demo by Kevin Medlock.

1988

    Group 70 is formed! Grand Opposition of Mars.

1989 (Vol. 1)

    Loma Prieta Earthquake, Desktop Publishing Comes to the Ephemeris!
